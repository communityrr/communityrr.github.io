Marketing Plan for Community Roofing & Restoration X Account

Daily Social Media Tasks and Strategy

  • Post Frequency: Aim to post at least once a day to keep your audience engaged.
  • Content Types: Share a mix of promotional content, educational posts, and community engagement.
  • Engagement: Respond to comments and messages within 24 hours to foster community interaction.
  • Analytics Review: Check analytics weekly to assess which types of posts perform best and adjust your strategy accordingly.
  • Hashtags: Use relevant hashtags to increase visibility. Research trending hashtags in your industry.
  • Visual Content: Incorporate images and videos in your posts to enhance engagement.
  • Collaborations: Partner with local businesses or influencers for cross-promotions.
  • Contests and Giveaways: Plan monthly contests to encourage user-generated content and increase followers.
  • Content Calendar: Curate and maintain a content calendar to plan and schedule posts in advance.

Customer-Focused Promotions Ideas

New Customer Welcome Discount

Offer: "15% off your first service for new customers."

Details: Apply the discount to any roofing inspection, repair, or restoration service. This helps ease new customers into trying your services and builds trust.

Promotion Ideas: Promote this on your website, social media, and local business directories. Use clear, welcoming messaging like, "Welcome to [Your Company Name]! Enjoy 15% off your first service!"

Seasonal "Storm-Ready" Specials

Offer: "Free roof inspection with any gutter cleaning service during storm season."

Details: Encourage customers to book preventative services before storm season with bundled offers. This is especially effective for spring or late summer when storms are more common.

Promotion Ideas: Schedule this promotion leading into peak storm months, targeting local homeowners with weather-based messaging like, ‚ÄúIs your roof storm-ready? Let us help!‚Äù

Refer-a-Friend Bonus

Offer: "$100 credit for each friend referred who completes a service."

Details: Make it easy for existing customers to refer friends and family by offering a direct credit on their next service or a gift card. This referral incentive is straightforward and effective.

Promotion Ideas: Make referral cards for customers or a digital referral form on your website. Send follow-up emails encouraging satisfied customers to refer friends.

"Roof Rescue" Contest

Offer: "Win a free roof inspection and maintenance package!"

Details: Run a seasonal or annual contest where customers can enter for a chance to win a free inspection and minor repairs. Use this to raise awareness and build leads.

Promotion Ideas: Use social media to promote entries, allowing customers to enter by following your page, tagging friends, or sharing a post. This spreads your brand visibility and creates engagement.

Holiday Specials

Winter Special: "20% off roof and gutter cleaning in December."

Spring Special: "10% off all inspections and maintenance for Spring Cleaning Month."

Storm Season Special: "Free tarp service with any storm damage inspection."

Promotion Ideas: Time these offers around key holidays or seasons to stay relevant and encourage preventive maintenance. Promote via social media, emails, and even local flyers to make sure the timing aligns with customers' needs.

Loyalty Discount Program

Offer: "Enroll in our loyalty program and get up to 10% off on every service."

Details: Reward returning customers by offering progressively larger discounts with each additional service booked (e.g., 5% for second service, 10% for third). It‚Äôs a way to build long-term relationships.

Promotion Ideas: Make it easy for customers to join, and promote this program when they complete a service to boost customer retention.

Community Partner Discounts

Offer: "Special discounts for veterans, teachers, and healthcare workers."

Details: Offer an exclusive 10% discount to veterans, teachers, and healthcare workers in your area as a way to give back and build goodwill.

Promotion Ideas: Mention this offer in your advertising materials, website, and social media as a way to support and engage with the community. You could also create partnerships with local schools or veterans' organizations to promote it.

First-Time Buyer Package

Offer: "First-time homeowners special: 10% off your first roofing service."

Details: Help new homeowners start on the right foot with a discount on their first inspection or repair service.

Promotion Ideas: Target this toward neighborhoods with a lot of new homeowners. Include a flyer in local welcome packets or offer this deal through real estate partnerships.
